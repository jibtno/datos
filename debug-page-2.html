Positioned strategically along Alabang-Zapote Road, this project provides easy access to major roads such as Skyway, South Luzon Expressway (SLEX), and MCX (Muntinlupa-Cavite Expressway), making it ideal for professionals, families, and investors alike. One Premier Features and Amenities One Premier offers a variety of features and amenities designed to enhance comfort and convenience for its residents. Administration Office Space: Provides easy access to building management and assistance for residents. 24-Hours Association Security: Round-the-clock security to ensure the safety of the community. Multi-purpose Function Room: A space for hosting gatherings, meetings, or events. Playground: An outdoor area for children to play and enjoy safe recreational activities. Viewing Deck: A vantage point offering scenic views of the surrounding area. 3 Elevators: Efficient and fast vertical transportation throughout the building. Fire Safety Features: Equipped with AFSS (Automatic Fire Suppression System), Fire Alarm System, Smoke Detectors, and Fire Exits with Pressurization Blowers. Water Storage System: Both overhead and underground water tanks ensure a steady and reliable water supply. Central Garbage Storage: Proper waste management facilities for the building. CCTV Surveillance: Security cameras installed in common areas to enhance the safety of residents. Sewage Treatment Plant: Ensuring the proper treatment and disposal of wastewater. Emergency Power Back-Up System for Common Areas: Providing electricity in case of power outages to ensure uninterrupted services in common areas. III. Why One Premier Is a Perfect Option One Premier stands out as an ideal choice for individuals and families seeking a well-balanced lifestyle. Its prime location in Las Piñas allows for easy access to key areas in the south of Metro Manila, including Alabang and Muntinlupa. The condominium’s proximity to business districts, educational institutions, and healthcare centers enhances the convenience of daily living. For those who value accessibility, One Premier offers unparalleled ease of commuting with access to major roadways like SLEX, Skyway, and MCX, ensuring a hassle-free drive to and from work or leisure destinations. Its amenities also cater to modern urban dwellers, with spaces designed for recreation, relaxation, and fitness. One Premier offers secure, comfortable living spaces that combine functionality and style, making it an excellent investment or long-term home. IV. One Premier Location One Premier is located at 1750 Alabang-Zapote Road, Almanza Uno, Las Piñas, Metro Manila. This prime location in the southern part of Metro Manila provides residents with a blend of city living and suburban tranquility. It is surrounded by commercial establishments, residential developments, and green spaces, giving it a balanced environment for those looking for modern conveniences without sacrificing peace and quiet. Nearby landmarks and destinations include: Alabang Town Center SM Southmall Festival Mall De La Salle Santiago Zobel School San Beda College Alabang Asian Hospital and Medical Center V. How to Get to One Premier To reach One Premier, follow these directions: From Visayas Avenue, head south and merge onto Quezon Avenue. Enter the Metro Manila Skyway heading toward Alabang. Stay on the Skyway, and exit onto Alabang-Zapote Road. Continue driving along Alabang-Zapote Road until you reach Barangay Almanza Uno, Las Piñas. One Premier will be on your right, easily accessible from the main road. The project is highly accessible via Skyway and SLEX, making it convenient for those coming from central Metro Manila, as well as neighboring areas in the south. VI. What are the Condominium Units in One Premier One Premier offers a selection of well-designed units to cater to different types of homeowners, including: Studio Units: Ideal for young professionals or individuals who prefer compact living spaces. 1-Bedroom Units: Suitable for couples or small families looking for additional privacy and space. 2-Bedroom Units: Perfect for growing families or those who require more room for comfort and flexibility. Each unit is thoughtfully designed to maximize space and functionality, with modern interiors that provide a comfortable and stylish living environment. Selected units offer expansive views of the surrounding Alabang area, adding to the charm of city living. VII.
